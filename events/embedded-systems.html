Talk on
Embedded Systems

30TH JANUARY 2023

Prof. Dhananjay Gadre was the invited speaker from NSUT, Netaji Subhas University of Technology. Prof Gadre is an expert on embedded systems and his course on embedded system design is also available on NPTEL platform of Swayam portal of the Government of India. He is author of six books and several professional articles. He is credited for establishing two open access Laboratories CEDT and Texas instruments Centre for Embedded product design( TI-CEPD). Under NEP, this course on embedded systems will be added as a core course for current and future batches of students. Since the second and third year students of BSc(H) Electronics would not have studied the said course as part of their syllabus, it was conducted for their benefit as well. The event was well attended by the students of the Department. Our college Principal supported the endeavour and addressed the students as well.✨️
